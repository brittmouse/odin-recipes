Odin Recipes - Black Bean Burgers

Description

If you're looking for a burger recipe that you can make for any vegetarian friends or family members you have, or if you just want to experiment, black bean patties are a surprisingly tasty way to go! This recipe was taken from another cookbook found at my workplace and I have not tested it, but the idea is pretty simple. Makes 6 patties.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry black beans
  • 1 cup farro
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1.2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 hamburger buns

Materials

  • Cutting board
  • Knives
  • Instant Pot

Directions

  1. Combine beans, farro, and water in the Instant Pot. Close lid and cook for 25 minutes.
  2. Place drained beans and farro in a bowl with onion, garlic, egg, breadcrumps, salt, and pepper. Mash together until well-combined. Shape mixture into patties.
  3. Wipe out the Instant Pot and set to Saute mode. Add oil. Cook the patties in batches until well-browned on both sides. Serve of buns with your choice of condimenta.

While this recipe uses the Instant Pot, you can do this using normal pots and skillets on a stove. Don't be afraid to experiment!
